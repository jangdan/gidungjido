controls

move around with the w a s d keys, shift to go up, space to go down.
move the mouse to look around, and scroll to zoom in and out.
press p to take a screenshot. you will be redirected to a seperate page with the screenshot. right click on it and select "Save Image As..."
press i to hide/show country information.
l: lock camera movement (drag to look around)
please remember to adjust the "overall height" and "height contrast" sliders and maybe change the theme below for each dataset for maximum visibility.
finally, press esc to toggle this menu.
